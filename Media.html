Wordpress

During my time working on Wordpress,we got to create a personal portfolio all about us. There were a few challenges I had while creating thsi website. The first one being making the layout work smoothly and blend together. Creating a website can get a little crazy and start to go all over the place, but once I got the hang of it, it started to get easier and easier.

Tips and Tricks!

  • Taking your time on figuring out your layout! If you just start throwing information on your website it can start to look cluttered and all over the place.
  • Use the layouts they give you. The layouts are there to help you make and organized website so use them!!
  • Make your desgin the same! Keep a theme going, don't get off track.
